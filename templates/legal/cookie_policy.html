1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security cookies: Protect against cross-site request forgery
  • Load balancing cookies: Ensure optimal website performance

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience on our website:

  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings (theme, language)
  • Authentication cookies: Keep you logged in between sessions
  • Shopping cart cookies: Remember items in your cart

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Google Analytics: Track page views, user behavior, and site performance
  • Custom analytics: Monitor specific features and user interactions
  • Performance cookies: Measure website speed and functionality

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements:

  • Targeting cookies: Show relevant ads based on your interests
  • Social media cookies: Enable sharing on social platforms
  • Retargeting cookies: Show ads for our services on other websites

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in and secure
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Understand how you use our website
  • Performance: Optimize website speed and functionality
  • Marketing: Show relevant content and advertisements
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time

Cookie Preference Center

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

6. Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for a set period (usually 1-2 years)
  • Essential cookies: Remain as long as necessary for functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Typically expire after 2 years

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features may not work properly:

  • You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some interactive features may not function
  • We won't be able to personalize your experience
